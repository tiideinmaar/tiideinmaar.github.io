I have been collecting typewriters since June of 2012. Currently, my collection consists of about 60 machines. A list of my collection with pictures and descriptions can be found below, as well as a more in depth list here. I think it goes without saying that Corona is by far my favorite brand of typewriter. Each image at the top of these typewriter pages is one of my prized Corona machines, edited to have that old time feel.
