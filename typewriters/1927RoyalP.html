TYPEWRITERS 1927 Royal P

Year: 1927

Make: Royal

Model: P

S/N: P26703

This machine is in fairly nice condition. A previous owner had spraypainted white over the original blue, and the new paint was badly peeling and cracking. Unfortunately, the original paint was unable to be restored. It was stripped off and repainted in cherry red. As far as I know, the black top pieces near the ribbons is original. I made sure to keep it intact as I repainted the rest.

At some point, I plan to add reproduction decals to this machine. I just don't have time at the moment.
