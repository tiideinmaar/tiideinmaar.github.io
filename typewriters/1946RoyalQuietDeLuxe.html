TYPEWRITERS 1946 Royal Quiet De Luxe

Year: 1946

Make: Royal

Model: Quiet De Luxe

S/N: -1248161

A beautiful machine. This machine has many aesthetic qualities that, I believe, lend it well to the title of the world's most beautiful typewriter. It has a touch of chrome which is not overpowering, but enough to give it a nice shine. The paint is matte, which makes it less distracting and gives nice contrast to the chrome. It is a simple machine, being relatively intuitive to use.

Regardless, it is not my favorite machine. That title belongs to the 1936 Corona Standard, Silent, or Sterling. In my opinion any of those may hold the title of the world's most beautiful typewriter.

Anyway, this machine was given to me by a family friend. She happens to refurbish old furniture for resale and occasionally acquires antiques such as this.
