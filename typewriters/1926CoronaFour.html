TYPEWRITERS 1926 Corona Four

Year: 1926

Make: Corona

Model: Four

S/N: 1K06940

This machine is in near mint condition. It is absolutely beautiful. As far as Coronas go, this is one of my favourites, second only to the 1936 Corona Standard. It seems to have the original ribbon covers, which is more than I can say for my 1925 Corona Four. The decals are stunning and the paint is nearly flawless.

This was given to me by a cousin for my high school graduation.
