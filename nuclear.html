Iowa State University - Nuclear Engineering Building

The Iowa State University Nuclear Engineering Building is currently undergoing a process of abating the asbestos within. This process will last until March 17, 2017. After this process is finished, demolition will begin on March 22, 2017. I will be entering the building between the 20th and 21st to take some final photographs before demolition. I will also be researching the history of the building throughout university archives, and update this page with any information I may find.
