TYPEWRITERS

Mac has been collecting typewriters since June of 2012. His collection currently includes somewhere around 40 to 50 different machines (he has lost count). A list of his collection with pictures and descriptions can be found below.

A more detailed listing of Mac's typewriter collection can be found here at the Typewriter Database.

Mac's collection spans many different makes of typewriter, including the following:

  • Corona
  • Smith-Corona
  • L.C. Smith Brothers
  • Royal
  • Halberg
  • Underwood
  • Elliot-Fisher
  • Olivetti
  • Remington
  • Woodstock

A list of individual machines is below.

  1. 1946 Royal Quiet DeLuxe

  2. 1925 Corona Four

  3. 1926 Corona Four

  4. 1954 Halberg Traveler

  5. 192X Elliot Fisher Accounting Typewriter

  6. 1946 Smith-Corona Silent
